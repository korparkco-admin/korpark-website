# korpark.com 이미지 생성 프롬프트 (claude.ai용)

아래 프롬프트를 claude.ai에서 하나씩 입력하여 이미지를 생성하세요.
모든 이미지는 **가로 1920x1080 비율**, **한국 남해안 분위기**, **블루 톤** 기조입니다.

---

## 1. 히어로 배경 (hero-bg.jpg)

```
Create a wide landscape photo (16:9 ratio) of a stunning aerial view of a Korean southern coastal area. A small drone is flying over turquoise waters with scattered green islands (Dadohae/다도해 style). The mood is cinematic with golden hour lighting. Blue-teal color tone. No text. Photorealistic style.
```

## 2. 드론 교육 (drone-education.jpg)

```
Create a photo of a modern drone training classroom inside a renovated Korean school building. Students are learning to fly small drones with a simulator screen on the wall. The room has clean white walls with blue accent lighting. A professional drone instructor is teaching. Bright, modern, educational atmosphere. Photorealistic.
```

## 3. 드론 낚시 (drone-fishing.jpg)

```
Create a dramatic photo of a waterproof drone hovering over the ocean near a Korean coastal breakwater (방파제). The drone has a fishing line hanging down into the blue water. Sunset lighting with orange and blue tones. A fisherman on the breakwater is controlling the drone with a remote. Cinematic, photorealistic style.
```

## 4. 캠핑장 (camping.jpg)

```
Create a cozy photo of an auto-camping site on a former school playground in rural Korea. Several tents and one camping car are set up on flat ground. String lights glow warmly at dusk. A school building is visible in the background, repurposed as an education center. Mountains and sea in the far distance. Warm, inviting atmosphere. Photorealistic.
```

## 5. 트레킹 (trekking.jpg)

```
Create a beautiful photo of a wooden deck coastal trail (해안 데크길) along the Korean southern coast. A couple is walking on the trail with ocean views and small islands visible. Lush green vegetation on both sides. Clear blue sky with a few white clouds. Fresh, healing atmosphere. Photorealistic.
```

## 6. 특산물 (local-produce.jpg)

```
Create a food photography style image of Korean Goheung local produce arranged on a rustic wooden table. Include: bright yellow yuzu (유자) fruits, red pomegranates (석류) cut open showing seeds, and fresh white onions (양파). Natural sunlight from a window. Clean, appetizing food photography style. No text.
```

## 7. 폐교 리모델링 컨셉 (school-renovation.jpg)

```
Create an architectural visualization of a Korean rural school building being renovated into a modern education and leisure center. The 2-story building has a fresh coat of white and blue paint. A sign area on the front (no text). The schoolyard has been converted to a camping area with a few tents. A drone landing pad is visible on the rooftop. Clean, hopeful, modern renovation feel. Photorealistic.
```

## 8. 레이저사격 (laser-shooting.jpg)

```
Create a photo of teenagers having fun in an indoor laser tag arena set up inside a converted Korean school classroom. Blue and red laser beams crossing the dark room with neon accent lights. Excited expressions on the players. Dynamic, fun, youthful atmosphere. Photorealistic.
```

## 9. 제주 카페리 (ferry-jeju.jpg)

```
Create a scenic photo of a white passenger ferry departing from a small Korean harbor (녹동항) heading towards the open sea. Cars are loaded on the deck. The harbor has a small lighthouse. Clear blue sky, calm sea. The mood suggests the start of an exciting journey. Photorealistic.
```

## 사용 방법
1. claude.ai에서 위 프롬프트를 하나씩 입력
2. 생성된 이미지를 다운로드
3. 파일명을 괄호 안 이름으로 저장 (예: hero-bg.jpg)
4. `E:\koreapark\korpark-website\images\` 폴더에 저장
5. 이후 index.html에 이미지 반영
